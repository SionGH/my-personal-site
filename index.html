I have made my home base in sunny Gran Canary Island. My tech journey started with a Master's in Graphic Design, followed by a diploma in UX Design. It was then that I fell for coding. I was hooked and wanted to learn more about how to take my design further and build my site.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I have made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my design skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
